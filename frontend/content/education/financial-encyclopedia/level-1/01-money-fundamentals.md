# Chapter 1: Money Fundamentals

## 1.1 What is Money?
Money is a tool that society agrees to use for exchanging value, measuring worth, and storing wealth.

### The Three Functions of Money
1. **Medium of Exchange**: Facilitates trade without the need for barter.
2. **Unit of Account**: Provides a standard measure of value (pricing).
3. **Store of Value**: Can be saved and used later, maintaining purchasing power.



## 1.2 The Time Value of Money (TVM)
A dollar today is worth more than a dollar tomorrow because money today can be invested to earn interest, and inflation reduces future purchasing power.
