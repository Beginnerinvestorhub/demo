# Chapter 5: Saving Money

## 5.1 Pay Yourself First
Prioritize saving by moving money to your savings or investment accounts immediately on payday before spending on anything else.

## 5.2 Emergency Funds
A buffer of 3-6 months of living expenses held in a liquid account to protect against unexpected financial shocks.
