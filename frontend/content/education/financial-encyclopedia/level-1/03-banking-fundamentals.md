# Chapter 3: Banking Fundamentals

## 3.1 Account Types
* **Checking Account**: Daily spending and bill payments.
* **Savings Account**: Short-term goals and emergency funds.

## 3.2 High-Yield Savings Accounts (HYSA)
An HYSA offers higher interest rates than traditional savings accounts, helping your money grow faster while remaining safe.
