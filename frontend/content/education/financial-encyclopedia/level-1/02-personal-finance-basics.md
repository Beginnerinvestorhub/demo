# Chapter 2: Personal Finance Basics

## 2.1 Income vs. Expenses
**Gross Income**: Total money earned before taxes and deductions.
**Net Income**: Your actual "take-home pay" used for budgeting.

## 2.2 The 50/30/20 Rule
* **50% Needs**: Housing, groceries, utilities.
* **30% Wants**: Hobbies, dining out, entertainment.
* **20% Financial Goals**: Savings, debt repayment, and investments.
