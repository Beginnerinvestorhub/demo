# Chapter 11: Stock Market Deep Dive

## 11.1 How Stock Markets Work
Stock markets are platforms where shares of publicly listed companies are traded. Prices fluctuate based on supply and demand, influenced by company performance and economic news.

## 11.2 Market vs. Limit Orders
* **Market Order**: Executes immediately at the best available current price.
* **Limit Order**: Sets a specific price at which you are willing to buy or sell. The trade only executes if the market hits that price.



## 11.3 Market Psychology
The market is often driven by two primary emotions: **Fear** and **Greed**. Understanding that markets move in cycles can help you stay disciplined during volatility.
