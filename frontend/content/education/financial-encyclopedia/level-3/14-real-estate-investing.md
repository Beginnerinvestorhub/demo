# Chapter 14: Real Estate Investing

## 14.1 Why Real Estate?
Real estate provides a unique combination of **Cash Flow** (rent), **Appreciation** (value increase), and **Tax Benefits** (depreciation).

## 14.2 Ways to Invest
1. **Direct Ownership**: Buying and managing rental properties.
2. **REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts)**: Buying shares of companies that own real estate; trades like a stock.
3. **House Hacking**: Living in one unit of a multi-family property while renting out the others.
