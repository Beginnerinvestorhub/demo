# Chapter 15: Tax Optimization

## 15.1 Tax Brackets Explained
The US uses a **Progressive Tax System**. You only pay higher rates on the portion of your income that falls within that specific bracket, not your entire income.



## 15.2 Tax Deductions vs. Tax Credits
* **Tax Deduction**: Reduces the amount of income you are taxed on (e.g., student loan interest).
* **Tax Credit**: A dollar-for-dollar reduction in the actual tax you owe (e.g., Child Tax Credit). Credits are generally more valuable than deductions.
