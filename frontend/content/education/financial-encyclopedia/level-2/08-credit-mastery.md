# Chapter 8: Credit Mastery

## 8.1 FICO vs. VantageScore
While both measure creditworthiness, they weight factors differently. FICO is used by 90% of top lenders.

| Factor | FICO Score |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Payment History** | 35% |
| **Credit Utilization** | 30% |
| **Length of History** | 15% |
| **New Credit** | 10% |
| **Credit Mix** | 10% |

## 8.2 Building Credit History
Effective techniques for beginners:
* **Secured Credit Cards**: Requires a cash deposit as collateral.
* **Credit-Builder Loans**: Loans designed specifically to build a payment history.
* **Authorized User**: Being added to a family member's established account.
