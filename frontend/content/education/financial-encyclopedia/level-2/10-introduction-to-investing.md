# Chapter 10: Introduction to Investing

## 10.1 Saving vs. Investing
* **Saving**: Focusing on safety and liquidity (short-term).
* **Investing**: Putting money to work to outpace inflation and build wealth (long-term).

## 10.2 Risk vs. Return
The higher the potential reward, the higher the risk. 
* **Stocks**: High risk, high growth.
* **Bonds**: Medium risk, steady income.
* **Cash/CDs**: Low risk, low return.



## 10.3 Diversification
"Don't put all your eggs in one basket." Spreading investments across different sectors and asset classes reduces the impact if one specific investment fails.
