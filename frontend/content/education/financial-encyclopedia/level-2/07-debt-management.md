# Chapter 7: Debt Management

## 7.1 Debt Snowball vs. Debt Avalanche
* **Debt Snowball**: Focuses on behavior. You pay off the smallest balance first to gain psychological "wins."
* **Debt Avalanche**: Focuses on mathematics. You pay off the highest interest rate first to save the most money over time.



## 7.2 Good Debt vs. Bad Debt
* **Good Debt**: Strategic debt used to buy assets that grow in value (e.g., mortgages, education).
* **Bad Debt**: High-interest debt used for things that lose value quickly (e.g., credit cards, payday loans).

## 7.3 Debt Consolidation
Combining multiple high-interest debts into a single, lower-interest loan to simplify payments and reduce interest costs.
